# Day 12 – AI-Powered Job Search & Personal Branding Toolkit

## Overview

Today I used Claude AI to build a complete Job Search & Personal Branding Toolkit based on my resume, target roles, and career goals.

The objective was to understand how AI can be used not only for resume optimization but also for personal branding, recruiter outreach, interview preparation, and career planning.

# Key Learnings

### ATS Optimization Matters

A technically strong resume can still fail ATS screening if keywords and formatting are not optimized.

### Projects Need Impact

Recruiters prefer:

* Problem Solved
* Technologies Used
* Results Achieved

instead of simple feature lists.

### Personal Branding Creates Opportunities

Building publicly through LinkedIn and GitHub increases visibility and credibility.
