Day 3 – Role-Based Prompting

Objective

Learn how assigning roles changes AI responses.

---

Prompt Used

How can I build an AI-powered study assistant for engineering students?

---

Response 1: No Role

Observation

Response 2: Founder Persona

How can I build an AI-powered study assistant for engineering students?

[Founder](founder.jpg) 

Observation

The response focused on business strategy, users, validation, and monetization.

Response 3: Developer Persona

---

Key Learnings

1. Role-based prompting changes AI behavior.
2. Founder personas provide business-focused insights.
3. Developer personas provide technical insights.
4. Better prompts lead to more useful outputs.
