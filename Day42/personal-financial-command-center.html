Tips, Prompts & Resources

💡 Quick tips
Pay yourself first. Move savings the day allowance arrives, not what's left over.
Track for 2 weeks. Most students underestimate small daily spends by 30–40%.
Beware subscription creep. ₹99/month feels small; ₹1,188/year is not.
🧭 Planning checklist mindset

Think in three horizons: this week (spending), this semester (goals), and this degree (skills that raise future income). Budgets fail when they only cover the first.

✦ AI prompts to try
"Review my last 10 expenses and flag any patterns"
"Explain compound interest using my SIP numbers"
"Suggest 3 ways to cut food spend without missing meals"
