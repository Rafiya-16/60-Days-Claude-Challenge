Priority Levels

  • Critical — resolve immediately, major KPI risk.
  • High — resolve soon, moderate risk.
  • Medium — manageable, but don't ignore for long.

Your Actions

Each alert offers several response options. Some are the smart operational call, some are costly overreactions, and some (like Ignore) are almost always risky. Choosing well raises Service Level, Customer Satisfaction, and Score. Choosing poorly raises Operating Cost and hurts your KPIs — sometimes a few seconds later, not instantly.
