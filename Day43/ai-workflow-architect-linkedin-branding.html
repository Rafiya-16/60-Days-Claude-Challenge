Workflow Summary

This workflow takes you from an unclaimed LinkedIn identity to a positioned, content-active profile that generates recruiter inbound. It runs on six stages — Positioning, Profile Optimization, Content Strategy, AI-Assisted Creation, Distribution & Engagement, and Tracking & Conversion — each with its own AI tool stack, prompts, and success checkpoints. Total realistic runtime to visible traction: 4–6 weeks at 3–5 hours/week, with content and networking becoming a lightweight weekly habit afterward.
