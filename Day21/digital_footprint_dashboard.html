Privacy Improvement Plan

Actionable steps ranked by estimated privacy score improvement. Estimates

1
Delete or restrict TikTok permissions
Revoke microphone, camera, and contacts access in device settings. Consider switching to a region-locked account or removing the app entirely. Highest single-action impact given cross-border data concerns.
+12 pts estimated
2
Enable Google Activity Controls
Pause Web & App Activity, Location History, and YouTube Watch History at myaccount.google.com. Set auto-delete to 3 months maximum.
+10 pts estimated
3
Switch to DuckDuckGo or Brave for search
Reduces search intent tracking by Google. Eliminates the most comprehensive behavioral dataset collector in your stack. Takes under 2 minutes to change default in phone settings.
+8 pts estimated
4
Review WhatsApp privacy settings
Set Last Seen, Profile Photo, and Status to "Contacts Only." Disable "Share data with Meta" under Settings → Privacy → Advanced.
+6 pts estimated
5
Turn off Instagram ad tracking
Settings → Ads → Ad Preferences → Data About Your Activity From Partners. Disable cross-site and cross-app tracking where available.
+5 pts estimated
6
Disable Google Photos face grouping
Google Photos → Settings → Face grouping → Off. Prevents biometric data from being derived from your stored photo library.
+4 pts estimated
